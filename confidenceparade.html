The Third Confidence Parade

By Maxixe2:252

It has already been almost two and a half cycles since the beginning of Era Three, which means it's almost time for the third annual confidence parade. We are expecting an even bigger turnout than in the last two parades, as more services, especially the Diamonds, have opened up to support Off Colors. What is confidence? It is being confident that we know best what forms are right for us, that we cannot be "fixed", and that we belong in this world! The parade will start at the Off Color Center at 0.15 cycles on 2:260, and then all around district 4-7. Non-Off Color supporters are welcome.
